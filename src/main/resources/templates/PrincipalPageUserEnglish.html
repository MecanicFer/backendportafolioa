Luis Fernando Armas Perez Java Developer


I am passionate about what I do, always looking for continuous improvement of processes, I like to be constantly learning new techniques, either to solve a technical problem or to acquire updated knowledge. Lover of cinema, music, martial arts and of course my family.

In the development of my professional career oriented to the maintenance of mechatronic systems, there are 3 fundamental areas that I like to define as the harmony between a mechanical system, an electronic system and a computer system, where one system does not work without the other, an example could be the operation of an automotive vehicle with Euro 6 standard, without going into great detail about the breadth of the subject, it has an internal combustion heat engine that includes controlled electronic injection, monitored and managed by software, this engine would not work without harmony of the 3 systems already mentioned.

In the development of my knowledge and skills in the aspect of mechanics of mechatronic systems, it undoubtedly led me to develop my knowledge in the area of ​​electronics and it did not take long for me to realize that I had to develop myself in the area of ​​informatics, specifically in software development. In the automotive world there are dominant languages ​​such as low level ones, in the process of my studies I came across the "Run One Run Anywhere" it was at that moment that I decided to specialize in the world of Java.
